Our Specialist Services

We are not a wig shop. We are specialists in creating fully customised hair solutions for individuals experiencing medical hair loss.

Personalised Consultations

Every journey begins with a free, private consultation where we understand your needs, preferences, and concerns in a supportive environment.

Custom Hair Pieces

Each wig and hair piece is individually customised to match your style, skin tone, and lifestyle. No two pieces are the same.

Toppers & Partial Solutions

Specialised toppers for targeted hair loss areas, providing natural-looking volume and coverage where you need it most.

Fitting & Styling

Expert cutting, styling, and fitting services to ensure your hair piece looks natural and feels comfortable.

Ongoing Support

Maintenance, adjustments, and care guidance throughout your journey. We're here for you every step of the way.

Hair Care Products

Specialised serums and care products designed specifically for maintaining your hair piece and supporting regrowth.

What Makes Tymeless Different

We combine technical expertise with genuine compassion to provide a service that goes beyond hair replacement.

Our Procedure

Our artisans meticulously sort and grade every strand by color and texture. Using our proprietary cleaning process, we restore hair to its purest form before crafting it into bespoke pieces. From Joico pigments to rigorous quality control, we ensure every wig, topper, and extension meets the highest standards of excellence.

Fully Customised

Every piece is individually created for you. We don't sell off-the-shelf products.

The Kindest Cut

We create pieces from donated hair and can even use your own hair for a truly personal solution.

How We Create Your Perfect Piece

Our process is designed to ensure you receive a hair piece that looks natural, feels comfortable, and restores your confidence.

1

Free Consultation

We meet with you privately to discuss your needs, show you options, and answer all your questions without any pressure.

2

Customisation

We take precise measurements and work with you to select colour, texture, length, and style that matches your vision.

3

Creation

Your piece is carefully crafted, using quality materials and, when possible, donated hair through The Kindest Cut program.

4

Fitting & Styling

We cut, style, and fit your piece to perfection, ensuring it looks and feels completely natural.

5

Ongoing Care

We provide maintenance guidance and remain available for adjustments, styling, and support throughout your journey.

Trusted by CANSA

We are proud to work alongside the Cancer Association of South Africa (CANSA) in supporting individuals through their cancer journey. As a trusted provider, we understand the unique needs of those experiencing medical hair loss and are committed to delivering compassionate, professional care.

Our partnership with CANSA reflects our dedication to providing quality, dignity, and hope to those navigating cancer treatment. We follow industry best practices and maintain the highest standards in crafting custom hair solutions that restore confidence and comfort.
