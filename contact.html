Head Measurement Guide

How to Measure:

  1. Circumference: Around the head where the wig will sit
  2. Front to Nape: From hairline to nape of neck
  3. Ear to Ear (across top): From above one ear to the other
  4. Ear to Ear (over front): Across the hairline
  5. Temple to Temple: Across the back of the head
  6. Nape of Neck: Width across the nape

Don't worry if you don't have measurements yet - we'll take them during your consultation!
